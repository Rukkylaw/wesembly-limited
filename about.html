STEPS TOWARDS A BETTER FUTURE

Helping to Modernize the Health System

Weesembly has been providing quality and timely medical diagnostic services to patients across Kwara state and beyond.

Our services include Healthcare services through the introduction of fetal velocimetry and report standardization for fetal biophysical profiling. This has improved the early diagnosis of fetal distress thereby substantially reducing the incidence of maternal mortality. Also development of local processes for different medical diagnostics procedures using the locally available resources to achieve desired results.Building and upgrading medical diagnostic facilities across the continent to meet minimum international standards and to provide a robust and round the clock diagnostic services to the public. Only efficient, quick and correct diagnosis can lead to good patients management and treatment. ICT services involves integration of multiple small and medium healthcare delivery facilities and providers to a network of diagnostic communication centres or central Medical Hubs through Digital Imaging and Communication in Medicine (DICOM) and Picture Achiving and Communication Systems (PACS).Also, facilitating the use portable, cheap and easy to use diagnostic equipment such as battery powered hand held ultrasound machines like the GE Vscan extend and Vscan air.
